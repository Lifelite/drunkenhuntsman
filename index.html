About Us

This community was started in 2016, officially on discord, when a group of friends who played games together grew tired of apps such as Skype, Curse, Teamspeak, etc. and moved to the app, Discord. Lifelite, being stuck with the chore of migration and setting up the server, decided upon the name "Drunken Huntsman"...a reference to Skyrim and a play on his surname. As time went on more and more friends of friends were invited and the community grew into what it is today.

This community is mostly 30+ year old gamers all with busy lives. With the state of the world, most are using Discord as their social network; a much more wholesome and fun way to keep up with friends, game together, and meme together. Now we have clans and groups across several games, feel free to look us up!
